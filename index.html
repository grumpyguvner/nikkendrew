Medicine and science needs to be accessible and there are some important messages that we need to highlight. The latest predictions are that 60% of us will be obese by 2050.

Our health is going to depend on major lifestyle changes. The future of high blood pressure treatment, osteoporosis management and heart disease isn't all about new medications.

Our diet and fitness are the key to delaying or preventing these potentially life threatening conditions. We need to learn from America's current situation and the strain on their health resources.

The sooner we start, the better. Too many of us are frightened to admit that we have a health problem, but once we recognise the problem we can do something about it.

I see TV as an ideal medium to help promote and educate the public on these and other important issues.

With my prior experience in the media, my science knowledge and my on-going medical commitments, I feel that I am ideally suited to present science and medical programming.
